INSTALLATION GUIDE

  1. Pull out your 3DS's SD Card, and load it up on a computer.
  2. Go to your "3DS" Folder on your SD card.
  3. Drag the contents of the "3ds" folder you downloaded Inj3ct0r, and place them in there.
